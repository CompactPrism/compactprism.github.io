Auto Ants 2

Ant colony simulator built in Unreal Engine 5. Simulate population control, send ants on missions for resources.

Key Features

  • Scout Missions: Send ants for a set amount of time and have them return with a chance to add missions to the mission board if they’re out for longer.
  • Gathering Missions: Send ants out on dangerous missions to gather resources. You can rush them and there’s a higher chance that they won’t return.
  • Building Rooms: Rooms allow for a higher capacity of ants and require multiple ants to build.
